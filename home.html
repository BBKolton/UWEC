A Luxury Contender

The University of Washington's EcoCAR team enters its second competition. After placing second among the country in its first competition, UW's EcoCAR is coming back to the competition with a retooled team, ready to take first place

What is University of Washington's EcoCAR?

The EcoCAR3 project is a 4-year competition among the highest prestige colleges and universities in America. Held by General Motors, EcoCAR3 builds on the proud 25-year history of the U.S. Department of Energy's Advanved Vehicle Competitions that power long lasting public/private partnerships, and give invaluable experience and training to promising students.

Run by True Leaders

Our team is made of students from the Mechanical, Electrical, Computer, and Chemical Engineering schools as well as the Business and Design schools. It takes all types to be successful in the EcoCAR3 competition.
